Why tournaments need huge bankrolls?

Large fields and top-heavy payouts create extreme variance, so even strong players can have long breakeven stretches before a few big scores drive results.

Bankroll requirements rise as fields grow and as ROI falls, and drop as a higher risk of ruin is tolerated, which is why MTT guidance commonly recommends triple-digit buy-in buffers.
