Tournament bankroll strategies

Choose a bankroll plan that matches your edge, volume, and how much risk of ruin you can accept. Larger fields create greater variance and usually require more buy-ins than small fields.

Conservative ≈1 % RoR

For beginners or anyone who cannot reload easily.

Field-size fit: Ideal for large-field MTTs (around 1,000+ entrants) where variance and long dry spells are most severe.

  • Buy-in cushion: 150 + (200 – 300 for the biggest fields)
  • Recommended ROI: 1 – 3 %
  • Survives 100-buy-in downswings but progresses slowly.

Standard ≈5 % RoR

The most common professional guideline for regular online schedules.

Field-size fit: Tuned for medium fields (about 200 – 1,000 runners) such as daily majors and many live dailies.

  • Buy-in cushion: 100 (move down if bankroll dips under ~75)
  • Recommended ROI: 5 – 10 %
  • Balances growth rate with reasonable protection from variance.

Aggressive 10 %+ RoR

For proven crushers willing to reload and tolerate wild swings.

Field-size fit: Best for small-field nightly MTTs or sit-and-gos (≤200 entries) where variance is lower and edges show up faster.

  • Buy-in cushion: 50 – 75 (drop down if roll falls below ~40)
  • Recommended ROI: 10 % +
  • Reaches higher stakes quickly but has a much higher bust probability.
