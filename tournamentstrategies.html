Tournament bankroll strategies

Choose a bankroll plan that matches your edge, volume, and how much risk of ruin you can stomach.

Conservative ≈1 % RoR

For beginners or anyone who cannot reload easily.
  • Buy-in cushion: 150 + (200–300 for 1,000-runner MTTs)
  • Recommended ROI: 1 – 3 %
  • Survives 100-buy-in downswings but advances slowly.

Standard ≈5 % RoR

The most common professional guideline for medium-field schedules.
  • Buy-in cushion: 100 (move down below ≈75)
  • Recommended ROI: 5 – 10 %
  • Good balance of growth and safety; still endures long breakeven stretches.

Aggressive 10 %+ RoR

For proven crushers who can reload and handle wild variance.
  • Buy-in cushion: 50 – 75 (drop down below ≈40)
  • Recommended ROI: 10 %+
  • Fast stake jumps but high bust probability and mental strain.
